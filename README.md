## ğŸ¯ What is QuestLabs Companion?

[cite_start]QuestLabs Companion is a mobile application designed specifically for **college students in cohort-based personal development programs**[cite: 2, 27]. It helps students bridge the gap between daily actions and long-term aspirations through a privacy-first, community-driven approach.

[cite_start]Unlike generic habit trackers, we integrate three critical dimensions of student life[cite: 27]:
* ğŸ§  **Emotional Wellness** - Track mood trends and emotional patterns.

## ğŸ“Š Research & Validation

Our design is strictly data-driven, based on two rounds of user studies.

### User Study 1: Requirements Gathering (n=14)
* [cite_start]**79%** of students demand check-ins under 2 minutes[cite: 235].

## ğŸ¨ Design Philosophy

### "Anti-Gamification" Approach
[cite_start]We intentionally removed anxiety-inducing mechanics based on our research[cite: 473]:
* ğŸš« **No Public Leaderboards:** Avoids unhealthy comparison.
